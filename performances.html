Performances

University of Mary Washington Chamber Music Festival

Saturday, February 25, 2017

The Chamber Festival is presented each Spring featuring student, faculty, and professional groups. Various musical styles and genres, spanning centuries, are performed. The Music Department is committed to promoting contemporary works. Original compositions by students and faculty are also debuted. This February, Soprano Monica Harwood and UMW's own Andrew Kraus will present the one-act chamber opera, Black Water by Jeremy Beck as a part of the festival.

All performances are in Pollard Recital Hall, room 304
All concerts, master classes, and recitals are free and open to the public and no tickets are required.
